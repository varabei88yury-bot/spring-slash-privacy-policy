Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the application. The application does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

1. Information Collection and Use

To improve application performance, stability, and gameplay experience, the app utilizes third-party developer services provided by Google (Firebase). These services automatically collect certain non-personal information when you use the app, including:

  • App Activity: Interaction data, screen views, and gameplay session metrics collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Performance & Diagnostics: Crash reports, stack traces, and error logs collected via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Device & Network Data: Device identifiers, operating system details, and approximate location derived from network IP addresses to understand regional usage trends.

2. Data Processing and Security

All data transmitted by the app's analytics and crash-reporting tools is encrypted in transit using standard HTTPS (TLS/SSL) protocols. This data is stored securely by Google and used strictly for diagnostic, analytical, and troubleshooting purposes.

3. User Choices & Opt-Out

You have control over your data. The application includes an in-app privacy and consent mechanism that allows you to opt out of data collection at any time. If you choose to opt out, analytics and tracking features will be disabled.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to external third parties. Data collected is processed exclusively through Google's developer services to maintain and optimize the app.

5. Children's Privacy and Age Limits

The app respects user privacy and complies with regional digital consent regulations. The minimum age for digital consent in our primary operating jurisdiction is 16 years old. The app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under this age without appropriate consent.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
